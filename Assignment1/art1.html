The Starry Night

Vincent van Gogh

1889

This masterpiece depicts a swirling night sky over a village, painted during van Gogh's stay at the Saint-Paul-de-Mausole asylum. The dynamic, dreamlike composition features bold brushstrokes and vibrant colors that capture the artist's emotional state and unique vision of the natural world.
