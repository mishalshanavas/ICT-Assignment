The Persistence of Memory

Salvador Dalí

1931

Dalí's most famous surrealist work featuring melting clocks in a dreamscape landscape. This iconic painting explores the relativity of time and the subconscious mind, combining precise realistic technique with impossible, dream-like imagery that challenges our perception of reality.
