Girl with a Pearl Earring

Johannes Vermeer

1665

Often called the "Mona Lisa of the North," this Dutch Baroque masterpiece captures an intimate moment with extraordinary use of light and color. Vermeer's masterful technique and the subject's direct gaze create an timeless sense of mystery and beauty that continues to captivate viewers centuries later.
